On the right side are the color sets which were shown to you previously. The color sets marked with black border are the ones you recalled. What does it indicate?

As can be seen,you recalled more color sets from the top and bottom than the middle rows. This shows the consonance with the Serial Position Effect. This shows the infuence of the Serial Position Effect.
